Orphan child runs away

By Cian Davey

Orphan Child ran away from Two Mills fearing bullies last night.

Jeffrey Magee, the kid who bunted the first frog ball, ran right out of Two Mills on the middle road separating East end and West end. Witnesses say he was being followed by the Cobras, a group of bullies that had hated Maniac ever since he arrived, and Mcnabs group, another group of bullies, and was forced out of Two Mills.
